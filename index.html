Pricing

Please find our standard pricing listed below.

Primary



  • Tutored by a qualified Primary School Teacher registered with the Qld College of Teachers (QCT)
  • Teaching stategy customised to the primary school level e.g. prep different to grade 6
  • For multiple sessions, tutor will liaise with primary teacher to create strategy
  • We have teachers that specialise in both academically gifted and challenged students
  • Tutors focus more on numeracy, literacy, digital literacy and scientific literacy rather than an exact curriculum
  • Subjects available for immediate tutoring include:
    • Maths
    • Technologies/ Coding
    • Science
    • English
  • Other subjects can be negotiated based on tutor availability


$50 per hour

Secondary



  • Tutored by a qualified Secondary School Teacher registered with the Qld College of Teachers (QCT)
  • Tutors are have experience teaching the Australian curriculum in the subject area
  • Teachers have formal qualifications related to the subject area being tutored
  • We have teachers that specialise in both academically gifted and challenged students
  • Subjects include:
    • All subjects from grade 7 to 10
    • All applied and authority-registered subjects in years 11 and 12 including VET
    • Year 11 and 12 Maths A/ Prevocational Maths
    • Year 11 and 12 English
    • Aquatic Practises and Biology
    • All Humanities, Business (except Accounting), Legal Studies, Design and Arts
    • We don't offer languages, music, manual arts, earth science and dance tutoring


$50 per hour

Secondary Specialist



  • Tutored by a qualified Secondary School Teacher registered with the Qld College of Teachers (QCT)
  • Tutors are have experience teaching the Australian curriculum in the subject area
  • Teachers have formal qualifications related to the subject area being tutored
  • Most Senior STEM subjects including:
    • 2018 Maths B and Maths C/ 2019 Maths Methods and Specialist Mathematics
    • Physics, Chemistry and Psychology
    • Digital Solutions
    • Accounting
    • Aerospace Systems and Engineering


$60 per hour

First Year Uni



  • Most STEM level 1 subjects including (but not limited to):
    • Maths up to Level 2 Calculus
    • All coding subjects
    • Organic and Inorganic Chemistry
    • Statistics and Epidemiology
    • Accounting
    • Introductory Physics
  • Tutors have both formal qualifications in the subject area being tutored finishing with a GPA over 6.0 and experience teaching


$65 per hour

Math Bootcamp



  • Available for students commencing Grades 10 to 12
  • Run in the December/January school holidays
  • Prepare for the next year of Maths education- particularly useful for those transitioning from Grade 10 to Senior Maths
  • Taught using predominantly non traditional teaching strategies proven to increase student engagement in Mathematics
  • Run over 10 days from 9am to 3pm
  • Meet like minded high performing students from the region

Group Tutoring Sessions

  • Minimum of 2, maximum of 5

  • Can be better than 1-on-1 tutoring because peers can think to ask questions that a student may overlook
